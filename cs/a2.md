### Ans

Answer the following:

1.  What does the domain registration pattern suggest about the organisation’s administrative maturity?

    >

        if
            long registration periods
            consistent registrars, nameservers
            auto renew enabled
        then
            organization's admin is mature

        if
            short registration periods
            multiple diffrent registrars, nameservers
            new expiry or missing domains
        then
            organization's admin is less mature

2.  Does the presence or absence of privacy protection change the target’s exposure? Explain.

    >

        Presence of privacy protection changes the target's exposure as its private informations is redacted (hidden via proxy) and not exposed.
        It limits direct targetting, email harvesting, social engineering entry points.

3.  What does an expiry date in the near future tell an attacker about a domain?

    >

        Having near future domain expiry date tells that
            possible admin negligence
            opportunity for attackers to take over domain

4.  If the registrant contact shows a named individual's personal email, what type of attack does this enable?
    >
        If personal email and contact is shown then following attacks are possibles
        1. Targetted phissing
        2. Social engineering
        3. Credentials harvesting
        4. Business email compromise
